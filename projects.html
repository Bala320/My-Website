1)Embedded C and Arduino

Automatic Water Level Controller:

In a group of 6 members including me , we built an automatic water sensor using embedded C language. Its a simple basic project
for a fresher. It works when the water level reaches the sensor inside the water tank , It Automatically turns of the Water motor
and Buzzes a sound for 10sec for the operator to switch off the motor.

Gadjets used:

  • Arduino UNO R3.
  • A Relay.
  • A Water motor.
  • A water sensor.
  • Some cnnecting wires.
  • A bucket.
